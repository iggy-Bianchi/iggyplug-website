PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 1, 2025

Overview

IggyPlug ("we," "us," or "our") operates IggyPlug.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. By using our site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including when you contact us by email, sign up for a plan, or correspond with us. This may include:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your Instagram handle and account details
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment providers)
  • Any other information you voluntarily share with us

We also collect basic usage data through standard web analytics tools, such as pages visited and time spent on the site. This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver the Instagram growth services you signed up for
  • Communicate with you about your account and service updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

Instagram Account Access

To deliver our growth services, we require access to your Instagram account information including your handle and niche details. We use this solely to perform the targeting and outreach activities described in our service agreement. We do not store your Instagram login credentials and do not retain account access beyond the scope of active service delivery.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. All email communications are conducted over encrypted connections. Payment information is never stored on our servers and is handled exclusively by our payment processor.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you cancel your plan, we retain basic records for up to 12 months for accounting and legal purposes, after which your personal information is deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at iggy@iggyplug.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies to ensure the site functions correctly and to understand aggregate usage. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active clients of material changes via email. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at iggy@iggyplug.com.
