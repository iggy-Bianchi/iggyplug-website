Who We Are

GROWTH THAT ACTUALLY MEANS SOMETHING

IggyPlug was founded by creators who were tired of watching their follower counts inflate with bots while their engagement rates tanked. We built the service we always wished existed: a team of real people doing real outreach, finding real followers who actually want to see your content.

We work with over 2,100 accounts across every niche you can think of. Fitness influencers, small businesses, podcasters, photographers, agencies, and everything in between. The common thread is that they all wanted growth they could be proud of.

Every account we manage gets a dedicated human being in its corner. Not a bot. Not a dashboard. A real person who knows your brand, understands your audience, and works every single week to connect you with the right people.
